In Vejle, part of the local Agenda 21 plan was to build the Ökolariet as a place where all citizens could come in and gain new knowledge about how it is possible to integrate sustainability into their daily lives

We opened our doors in the autumn of 2003 and today receive approximately 90,000 guests per year, of which approximately 15,000 are students who attend the Ökolariet school service.

Økolariet is part of the circle of Science Education Activity Centers in Denmark (VPAC) and we are a member of the Nordic Science Center Association (NSCF).

The sunflower cord symbolizes a hidden disability. These may include challenges related to pain, learning, hearing, speech or mental illness.

The sunflower necklace is delivered free of charge while supplies last and no questions asked.

You can read more about the sunflower string at solsikkeprogrammet.dk
