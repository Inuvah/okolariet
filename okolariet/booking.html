Presentation during opening hours

If you come as a group, it is possible to book a presentation of approx. 30 minutes about the Økolariet or one of the subjects we treat in our exhibitions. Afterwards, there is an opportunity to walk around the exhibitions on your own. The price is DKK 1,000 incl. VAT (possibility of discount for schools and institutions) The presentations are offered during our opening hours at 10-16, and there can be max. be 30 people for a presentation. 

Presentations offered:

Knowledge of the Økolariet: The idea behind the Økolariet and introduction to the concept of sustainability, as well as a short introduction to the exhibitions. 

Energy and climate: Get an overview and background knowledge about climate change. Why is the climate changing, where do we get our knowledge from, and what can we do about it?

Diet and climate: How can we make our diet more climate-friendly and sustainable, and what is the connection between diet and climate?

Exclusive tour outside opening hours

If you would like to have the Økolariet to yourself, we offer a guided tour of the Økolariet's exhibitions after normal opening hours, with stories about all the different subjects and elements you encounter. The tour lasts approx. 1.5 hours, and there can be max. 15 people for the tour. The offer can be combined with a presentation on the Økolariet and the concept of sustainability (approx. 30 min.). The price is DKK 2,500 incl. VAT. If a presentation on the Økolariet and the concept of sustainability is selected, the price is DKK 3,000 incl. VAT.

Nature tours with Økolariet

Økolariet offers year-round guided trips into Vejle's varied and beautiful nature. The tours are free and they are organized for families and individuals. If you want to go on a trip with a school class or another type of group, you must contact the nature guide to arrange a separate arrangement.  Some of the tours have a limited number of participants and require registration. You register online here on the website.Økolariet reserves the right to cancel events where there are less than 5 participants registered the day before. The trips typically last a few hours and are usually 3-4 km.  The trips are taken at a moderate pace and there are a number of stops along the way. The tours will normally be challenging for people with walking difficulties and for example prams, as we walk on forest roads and along small footpaths, uphill and makeshift stairs, through gates, in the forest floor and terrain etc. Remember footwear that suits the season and the weather - it can be wet and muddy.
